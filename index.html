My Publications

A systematic review of deep learning data augmentation in medical imaging: Recent advances and future research directions.

Short Desrciption : The systematic review covers deep learning data augmentation, datasets, and evaluation metrics in medical imaging. It discusses traditional data augmentation methods and explores future research directions. Additionally, it provides a comprehensive evaluation of recent state-of-the-art research, addressing challenges in deep learning-based data augmentation and outlining future research directions. The review also highlights the integration of multimodal data to enhance the comprehensiveness of medical imaging analysis.
Authors : Tahuhidul Islam, Md Sadman Hafiz, Jamin Rahman Jim,Md Mohsin Kabir, M.F. Mridha
Publisher : Elsevier
Journal Name : HealthCare Analytics(Scopus Indexed , Q2)
Date: 21 May 2024

Tea leaf age quality: Age-stratified tea leaf quality classification dataset

Short Description: The “Tea Leaf Age Quality” dataset, with 2208 raw images from Bangladesh's tea gardens, classifies tea leaves into four age-based quality classes (T1 to T4). It aids in tea leaf classification, detection, and quality prediction, supporting advanced research and agricultural technological evolution. Authors : Md Mohsin Kabir, Md Sadman Hafiz, Shattik Bandyopadhyaa, Jamin Rahman Jim,, M.F. Mridha
Publisher : Elsevier
Journal Name : Data in Brief(Scopus Indexed , Q2)
Date: 21 April 2024
